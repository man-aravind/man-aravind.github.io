Full Name Manaoj Aravind Vivekanandan

Research Interests

  • Dynamical Systems | Chaos Computing | Stochastic Resonance | Nonlinear Electronics

  • Application of nonlinearity and emergent phenomena to yield better design.

    My PhD work involved using the interplay of noise and nonlinearity to make better logic gates. At present I study emergent behavior and control of coupled nonlinear systems, using both simulations and experiments.

    I’m deeply passionate about using complexity for better computation and most of my work has revolved around that theme. I'm looking for new avenues to expand the scope of my work.
